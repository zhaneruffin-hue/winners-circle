Our Origin Story

How WINNERS Tampa Came to Be

WINNERS Tampa was founded in memory of Jordan Jolly, a local kid who fell in love with lacrosse at nine years old and let the sport shape him into a confident, disciplined young leader before his unexpected passing in 2019.

To honor him, his parents, Carol and Lew Jolly, partnered with WINNERS DC to bring the program home to Tampa. Carol, who spent her career as a Deputy Sheriff serving the community, saw firsthand what kids in under-resourced neighborhoods were up against — and she and Lew wanted Jordan's love of the game to open a door for other families who might never otherwise get the chance.

Nearly a third of children in the Tampa Bay area grow up in poverty, often with little access to safe after-school activities or positive mentors. WINNERS Tampa exists to close that gap: free lacrosse programming paired with mentorship, character development, and a supportive community that helps local youth build confidence, discipline, and life skills that carry far beyond the field.
